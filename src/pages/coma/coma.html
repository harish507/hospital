Hepatic Coma

Introduction

Hepatic coma in the advanced stages of non-cirrhotic patients equitable occurs because of lack of ability of the liver to get rid of various toxins nitrogen and ammonia

In the early stages of a coma:

  1. The work of an enema to the patient every 6 hours.
  2. Use Allaktayloz rate of 2 tbsp every 8 hours.
  3. Use Alnaoamasin tablets at a rate of 2 tablet every 8 hours.
  4. Stop taking diuretics medications.
  5. Prevent eating foods rich in animal protein

In case of severe coma:

The patient should be transferred to the hospital where the patient in this case needs for medical care and private nursing, and also to determine the cause of the coma as some cases coma may be accompanied by internal bleeding from esophageal varices.
