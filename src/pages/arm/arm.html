Arms Worksouts

Arm Raises

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Stand on the floor with your arms extended straight forward at shoulder height.

Step 2:Raise your arms above your head. Return to the start position and repeat.

Triceps Dips

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:For the start position, sit on the chair. Then move your hip off the chair with your hands holding the edge of the chair.

Step 2:Slowly bend and stretch your arms to make your body go up and down. This is a great exercise for the triceps.

Arm Circles

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Stand on the floor with your arms extended straight out to the sides at shoulder height.

Step 2:Move your arms clockwise in circles fast. Try to do it as fast as you can.

Step 3:It's a great exercise for the deltoid muscle.

Diamond Push-Ups

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Start on all fours with your knees under your butt and your hands under your shoulders.

Step 2:Make a diamond shape with your forefingers and thumbs together directly under your face, then push your body up and down.

Step 3:Remember to keep your body straight.

Chest Press Pulse

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Hold your forearms together at shoulder height and bend your elbows with your hands together to make an 'L' shape.

Step 2:Then lift your forearms up and down.

Jumping Jacks

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Start with your feet together and your arms by your sides,then jump up with your feet apart and your hands overhead.

Step 2:Return to the start position then do the next rep.

Step 3:This exercise provides a full-body workout and works all your large muscle groups

Diamond Plank

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Start in the straight arm plank position.

Step 2:Lift your right arm and left leg until they are parallel with the ground.

Step 3:Return to the start position and repeat with the other side.

Pushups

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Lay prone on the ground with arms supporting your body.

Step 2:Keep your body straight while raising and lowering your body with your arms.

Step 3:This exercise works the chest, shoulders, triceps, back and legs.

Wall Pushups

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Stand in front of a wall one big step away from it.

Step 2:Then put your hands out straight towards the wall and lean against it.

Step 3:Slowly bend your elbows and press your upper body towards the wall. Push back and repeat the exercise.
Remember to keep your body straight.

Dumbell Wrist Twist

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1: Stand with Light Dumbbell in each hand with your arms at your sides, palms facing behind you.

Step 2:Bend your arms to curl the weights up until your forearms are parallel to the floor. This is the starting position.

Step 3: Rotate your wrists until your palms face the ceiling, then back so your the floor once again. That's one repetition.

Diamond Pushup On Bench

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1: Place yourself in pushup position but have your hands placed directly under your chest with your index fingers and thumbs spread and touching so they form a diamond Keep the toes of your feet on a flat bench. This will put your hands while performing the pushups.

Step 2: Keep your back flat as you lower your body until your chest is nearly touching your hands.

Step 3: Pause, then push yourself back up to the starting position.

Seated Triceps Extension

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1: Sit on a bench, and grab pair dumbbells. Hold them so that they are at arm's length above your head with palms facing inward together.

Step 2: Keep your upper arms stable as you lower the dumbbells behind your head until your forearms are just past parallel to the floor.

Step 3: Pause, then straighten your arms to return the dumbbells to starting position.
