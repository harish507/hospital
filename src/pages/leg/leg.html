Squats

Click Here  to know more.


Step 1:Stand with your feet shoulder width apart and your arms stretched forward, then lower your body until your thighs are parallel with the floor.

Step 2:Your knees should be extended in the same direction as your toes. Return to the start position and do the next rep.

Step 3:This works the thighs, hips buttocks, quads, hamstrings and lower body.

Side Lying Leg Lift

Click Here  to know more.


Step 1:Lie down on your side with your head rested on your left arm. Lift your upper leg up and return to the start position.

Step 2:Make sure your right leg goes straight up and down during the exercise.

Step 3:It's a great exercise for the gluteus.

Backward Lunge

Click Here  to know more.


Step 1:Stand with your feet shoulder width apart and your hands on your hips.

Step 2:Step a big step backward with your right leg and lower your body until your left thigh is parallel to the floor. Return and repeat with the other side.

Donkey Kicks

Click Here  to know more.


Step 1:Start on all fours with your knees under your butt and your hands under your shoulders.

Step 2:Then lift your left leg and squeeze your butt as much as you can. Go back to the start position and repeat the exercise.

Knee-Chest Stretch

Click Here  to know more.


Step 1:Lie on the floor with your legs extended. Lift your right knee up and grab it with both hands.

Step 2:Pull your right knee towards your chest as much as you can while keeping your left leg straight on the ground.

Step 3:Hold this position for a few seconds.

Step 1:Stand straight with your hands on the wall and feet shoulder width apart.

Step 2:Lift your heels and stand on your toes. Then drop your heels down. Repeat the exercise.

Sumo Squat Calf Raises

Click Here  to know more.


Step 1:Stand with your hands on the wall and your feet a little wider than shoulder width apart.

Step 2:Lower your body until your thighs are parallel to the floor. Lift your heels up and down.

Single Leg Squat

Click Here  to know more.


Step 1: Stand with your feet shoulder width apart. Lift your stronger or dominant leg straight out in front of your, as high as possible.

Step 2: Such air into the stomach, then push your hips back and squat down as far as possible while keeping your torso as upright as possible. The heel of your front leg can touch the floor at the bottom of the squat, if you can get down that far.

Step 3: Return to the starting position and exhale. Do all your repetitions with your non dominant leg, then repeat with your dominant leg.

Dumbell Side Lunge

Click Here  to know more.


Step 1: Standing upright, hold a pair of dumbbells by your side with arms extended downward

Step 2: Step to one side and lower your body as your lean your torso slightly forward. Keep the stationary leg straight throughout the movement.

Step 3: Push off of the bent leg to return to the starting position. Repeat with the other leg.
