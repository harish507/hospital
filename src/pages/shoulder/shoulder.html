Shoulder & Back Worksouts

Knee Push-Ups

Step 1:Start in the regular push-up position, then let your knees touch the floor and raise your feet up off the floor.

Step 2:Next push your body up and down.

Rhomboid Pulls

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Stand with your feet shoulder width apart.

Step 2:Raise your arms parallel to the ground, and bend your elbows. Pull your elbows back and squeeze your shoulder blades.

Step 3:Repeat this exercise.

Jumping Jacks

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Start with your feet together and your arms by your sides,then jump up with your feet apart and your hands overhead.

Step 2:Return to the start position then do the next rep.

Step 3:This exercise provides a full-body workout and works all your large muscle groups

Arm Raises

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Stand on the floor with your arms extended straight forward at shoulder height.

Step 2:Raise your arms above your head. Return to the start position and repeat.

Side-Lying Floor Stretchs

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Lie on your left side with your left & right knee slightly bent in front of you and your right leg stretched behind the left leg.

Step 2:Straighten your right arm over your head and gently pull on your right & left wrist to stretch the right side of your body.

Step 3:Hold this position for a few seconds.

Arm Scissors

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1: Stand upright with your feet should width apart. Stretch your arms in front of you at shoulder height with one arm overlap the other in the shape of the letter “X”, and then spread them apart.

Step 2:Switch arms, and repeat the exercise.

Cat Cow Pose

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Start on all fours with your knees under your butt and your hands directly under your shoulders.

Step 2:Then take a breath and make your belly fall down, shoulders roll back and head come up towards the ceiling.

Step 3:As you exhale, curve your back upward and let your head come down. Repeat the exercise.

Do it slowly with each step of this exercise.

Pushups

Step 1:Lay prone on the ground with arms supporting your body.

Step 2:Keep your body straight while raising and lowering your body with your arms.

Step 3:This exercise works the chest, shoulders, triceps, back and legs.

Wall Pushups

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1:Stand in front of a wall one big step away from it.

Step 2:Then put your hands out straight towards the wall and lean against it.

Step 3:Slowly bend your elbows and press your upper body towards the wall. Push back and repeat the exercise.
Remember to keep your body straight.

Chinups

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1: Choose a suitable weight, then kneel dr. stand on the platform and grip the handles. Keep you abdominals and lower back muscles strong and slowly extend your arms until they are fully stretched Make sure that you keep your shoulders down and retracted slightly back this will help to keep the tension on your back muscles.

Step 2: Keeping the movement under control and your elbows pulled down and back pull yourself back up to the start position. Repeat for the desired number of reps.

Dead Lifts

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1: Stand with your feet hip-distance apart and bend down in sitting motion until your thighs are parallel to the floor. Keep your back straight and make sure that your spine is as close to the neutral position as possible. Take an overhand grip on the bar with your hands slightly wider than a shoulder-width apart.

Step 2: Exhale, draw your abdominal muscles in, and lift the bar by pushing up throliga your legs.

Step 3: As the bar reaches your knees during the lift phase, push your hips forward to raise your torso so that you are Standing tall with your arms by your sides and the bar resting on your thighs.

Step 4: Hold the position for two seconds, inhale and return the weight to the floor.

Dumbell Shrungs

Click Here  to know more.

Step 1: Stand upright and hold two fairly Heavy dumbbells at your sides, with your palms facing each other (neutral-grip).

Step 2: Keep your shoulders relaxed Shrug your shoulders as if you were trying to touch them to your ears.

Step 3: Hold the top most position, then gradually lower them to the starting position. Do not bend your elbows or shift your head forward during the motion. Repeat.
